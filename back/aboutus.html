Who are we?

We are a group of students from the University of Puerto Rico at Mayagüez.

We are currently taking the course of Software Engineering (COMP 4025) with professor Juan Carlos Ortiz.

We are a group of 5 students, each one with a different role in the development of this game.

We are all very excited to be working on this project and we hope you enjoy it as much as we do.
